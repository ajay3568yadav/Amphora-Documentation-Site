Welcome to Amphora's documentation!
===================================

**Amphora** is a platform designed for the management and sharing of scientific data. 
It provides researchers with a secure, cloud-based repository to store, access, and share their data, 
as well as tools for collaboration and analysis. The platform is specifically designed for scientific data, 
with features that facilitate metadata management, versioning, and reproducibility. Amphora is widely used in 
the scientific community, particularly in the life sciences and biotech industries, and offers a range of 
pricing plans to accommodate the needs of researchers and organizations of all sizes.

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
